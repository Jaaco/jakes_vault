### ExerciseM

the standard model for exercises

### ExerciseDraftM

the model that is used to hold the exercise data during crud operations
this can be created from and converted to an ExerciseM

### Equipment

this is hard coded into the app (not in database)
includes things as:
Barbell
Machine
Bodyweight

could be extended in the future
could be used in preferences to filter exercises (eg. person only has bodyweight and barbell in home gym)

## Movement Type

this is used to describe how 'big' an exercise is
small - medium -large
range from pure isolation to full body compound
